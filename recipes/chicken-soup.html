Chicken Soup

Home

This chicken soup recipe is a classic and a family favorite.


This chicken soup is a real "crowd-pleaser" and tends to go fast. It's a great way to get everyone to eat more vegetables. You can use any type of vegetables you like, but the ones we will use are just a suggestion.


Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 cup celery, chopped
  • 1 cup carrots, chopped
  • 1 cup green beans, chopped
  • 1 cup corn kernels
  • 1 cup peas
  • 1 cup potatoes, chopped
  • 1 cup tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 cup zucchini, chopped
  • 1 cup yellow squash, chopped
  • 1 cup bell peppers, chopped
  • 1 cup spinach, chopped
  • 1 cup kale, chopped
  • 1 cup leeks, chopped
  • 1 cup garlic, minced
  • 1 cup ginger, minced
  • 1 cup turmeric, minced
  • 1 cup salt
  • 1 cup pepper
  • 1 cup water

Directions:

  1. Place the chicken in a large pot and cover with water. Bring to a boil and then reduce the heat to a simmer. Cook for 1 hour.
  2. Remove the chicken from the pot and set aside to cool. Once cool enough to handle, remove the meat from the bones and chop into bite-sized pieces.
  3. Return the chicken to the pot and add the onion, celery, carrots, green beans, corn, peas, potatoes, tomatoes, zucchini, yellow squash, bell peppers, spinach, kale, leeks, garlic, ginger, turmeric, salt, and pepper. Add water if needed to cover the vegetables.
  4. Bring to a boil and then reduce the heat to a simmer. Cook for 30 minutes or until the vegetables are tender.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
