Shrimp Spaghetti

Home

This shrimp spaghetti recipe is a classic and a family favorite.


This shrimp spaghetti is a real "crowd-pleaser" and tends to go fast. It's a great way to get everyone to eat more seafood. You can use any type of seafood you like, but the ones we will use are just a suggestion.


Ingredients:

  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 1 pound shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup parsley, chopped
  • 1/4 cup basil, chopped
  • 1/4 cup oregano, chopped
  • 1/4 cup salt
  • 1/4 cup pepper
  • 1/4 cup water

Directions:

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil and butter over medium heat. Add the garlic and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  3. Add the shrimp and cook until pink, about 2-3 minutes per side.
  4. Add the cooked spaghetti to the skillet and toss to combine.
  5. Add the parsley, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Toss to combine.
  6. Add the water and toss to combine. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes.
